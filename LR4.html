Wooden Spoon

About Their Store:

WOODEN SPOON is the Filipino restaurant of Chef Sandy Daza,
the heir apparent of the legendary Nora Daza who created the
Filipino Food Cookbook bible for newly married couples and
for Filipinos living abroad. The resto is named after the
signature wooden spoon he used in his cooking shows.
